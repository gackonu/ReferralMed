Our Mission

We want to simplify the process of compensating healthcare professionals and non-healthcare professionals who recommend patients to convenient facilities.

If necessary, we would call, pick up, and take patients to the best facility available. Additionally, we want to boost the number of visitors to the facility by driving more traffic your way in exchange for a flexible commission based on the cost. Paying the referrer a share, which will encourage them to continue submitting cases our way.
